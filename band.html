The Band

Adrian Vale

Background: Hailing from Detroit, Michigan, Adrian grew up surrounded by the sounds of rock, finding his voice at various open-mic nights. His passion for music comes from his late father, a blues guitarist who taught him the power of raw emotion in every note.

Role: Lead Singer

@adiranVale43

Cass Reed

Background: Growing up in Asheville, North Carolina, Cass was influenced by folk music and classic rock albums he discovered in her youth. His passion for music came early in life as he started playing the keyboard at age 10.

Role: Keyboard Player

@cassReed75

Elias Crowe

Background: Born in New York City, Elias grew up immersed in classical music, with his mother being a concert pianist and his father a composer. His passion for the cello began at 8 years old, drawn to its rich, expressive sound.

Role: Cellist

Felix Thorne

Background: From New Orleans, Louisiana, Felix was immersed in the rich jazz culture of his hometown.. With a passion for blending rock and jazz, he uses his improvisational skills to weave classical and rock elements into his saxophone solos, creating a distinct fusion of genres.

@felixThorns

The Early Days

The Phantom Serenade began with lead singer Adrian Vale and keyboardist Elias Crowe who met working backstage at a local theater in Denver. During their late-night shifts, they bonded over music and began blending their shared love for rock music. After hours, they used the empty stage to rehearse, shaping the foundation of their sound.

They soon brought in drummer Cass Reed, whose sharp playing gave their music a stronger presence. The final piece was our talented saxophonist Felix Thorne, who had a background in jazz but was drawn to their style. With the group established they played small venues, slowly building a loyal audience.
